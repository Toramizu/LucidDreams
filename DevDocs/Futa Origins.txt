The legends tell of an angel who came to this world to save it from evil. After purifying it from powerful demons, she stayed here to make sure no other would try to taint it. And during that time, the angel found love with a woman. The two of them had a child, half human, half angel and of both genders, who they named Futanari, meaning "Of two kinds".
The angel had a lot of followers and was treated like a queen, so when the time came for her to go back to the Sky, her daughter was appointed as her heiress and was a virtuous queen. Her children, then her children's children would be hermaphrodites too.
